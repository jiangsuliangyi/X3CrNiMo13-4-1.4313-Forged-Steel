Welcome to the technical repository for **X3CrNiMo13-4 (1.4313)** martensitic stainless steel forgings. This material, often referred to as **F6NM**, is engineered for high-stress environments requiring a balance of extreme strength, low-temperature toughness, and superior corrosion resistance.

---

## 🔍 Material Overview

**X3CrNiMo13-4** is a low-carbon martensitic steel with significant additions of Nickel (3.5–4.5%) and Molybdenum (0.3–0.7%). Unlike standard martensitic grades, its unique chemistry provides:
*   **Superior Weldability:** Reduced carbon content minimizes the risk of cold cracking.
*   **High Ductility:** Excellent impact strength even at sub-zero temperatures.
*   **Erosion Resistance:** Highly resistant to cavitation, making it ideal for hydraulic machinery.

## 🏗️ Industrial Applications

Our 1.4313 forged parts are widely utilized in critical sectors:
1.  **Hydropower:** Turbine blades, Pelton wheels, and Francis runners.
2.  **Oil & Gas:** High-pressure valve bodies, compressor shafts, and pump internals.
3.  **Chemical Engineering:** Centrifuge components and pressure vessel forgings.
